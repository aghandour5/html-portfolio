The Best Movies According to Ali

My top 3 movies of all-time

Extraction 2

Extraction captivates with its intense action sequences, compelling storytelling, and Chris Hemsworth's powerful performance, making it one of the best movies in its genre

Sniper

Sniper stands out for its gripping sniper duels, tense atmosphere, and sharp character dynamics, delivering a thrilling and suspenseful military drama.

Hobbs & Shaw

Hobbs & Shaw impresses with its high-octane, over-the-top action, charismatic performances from Dwayne Johnson and Jason Statham, and a perfect blend of humor and spectacle, solidifying its status as an exhilarating and entertaining spin-off in the Fast & Furious franchise.
